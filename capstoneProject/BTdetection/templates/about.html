About Us

GNN Tumor Detection is a web-based tool designed to help individuals detect potential brain tumors from MRI scans using cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence. Our model leverages Graph Neural Networks (GNN) to analyze complex spatial features within MRI data, providing fast and reliable feedback.

Our mission is to make early tumor detection accessible and understandable to everyone – not just doctors or researchers. Whether you are concerned about your health or monitoring a loved one, our platform brings peace of mind with just a few clicks.
